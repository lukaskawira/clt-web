CV Cisarua

Established in 1949, CV. Cisarua is an Indonesian leather tannery that played a pivotal role in the domestic production of mid to high-end crash and finish leather, establishing us as one of the pioneers of in-house leather manufacturing in the country.

Profound Knowledge in Leather

Since our tannery's inception, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to preserving and applying traditional leather craftsmanship techniques.

Quality and Environmental Policies

Our company consistently strives to deliver top-notch products while simultaneously prioritizing responsible waste management practices.

74 Years of Trust

Over the years, our expertise and track record of success in the leather industry have earned us more than 100 partners and clients from all corners of Indonesia and around the globe, including government institutions, automotive manufacturers, and apparel companies.

By combining modern technological advancements with traditional leather crafting techniques, we have experienced significant growth in the demand for our leather products both domestically and in international markets.

Our state-of-the-art facility, located in the heart of West Java, is one of the premier modern facilities in Indonesia. Thanks to a reliable supply of raw materials from reputable local and overseas suppliers, we have been able to maintain operations while consistently satisfying our past clients and present partners.

Our aim is not only to satisfy our customers' needs with our high-quality products, but we also prioritize compliance with environmental regulations and strive for energy and resource conservation, waste reduction, and recycling.
